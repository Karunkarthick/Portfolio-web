Yes, I Have Two Projects are Plant Disease Prediction and Diagnosis and Graph Based Search Engine Agricultural sustainability hinges on early detection and management of plant diseases, which often go unnoticed until crop damage becomes irreversible. This project introduces a hybrid system that combines deep learning-based plant disease prediction with a graph-based search engine, enabling intelligent diagnosis and contextual exploration of plant health data. The disease prediction module leverages Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and pre-trained models like VGG16 and ResNet to classify leaf images into disease categories such as apple scab, maize rust, and tomato yellow leaf curl. With a testing accuracy exceeding 96%, the system empowers farmers and agronomists to take timely action, reducing crop loss and improving yield. To enhance usability and knowledge discovery, the system integrates a graph-based search engine powered by Apache AGE, a PostgreSQL extension for graph databases. This engine models plant species, symptoms, treatments, and environmental factors as interconnected nodes and relationships. Users can traverse the graph to: - Discover related diseases across crops - Identify treatment protocols based on symptom clusters - Explore environmental triggers linked to disease outbreaks By combining image-based diagnosis with semantic graph traversal, the platform offers a holistic tool for precision agriculture. It transforms static data into an interactive knowledge graph, enabling researchers, farmers, and policymakers to make informed decisions grounded in both visual evidence and relational context.
